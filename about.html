💬 Short Introduction:
Anton is a Korean-American artist and a proud member of RIIZE under SM Entertainment. Known for his calm aura, gentle voice, and love for music, Anton has charmed fans worldwide. Before becoming an idol, he was a successful swimmer, having competed in various championships during his youth. He is also the son of the legendary music producer Yoon Sang, which makes his deep connection to music feel almost natural.

With a strong sense of identity and quiet charisma, Anton is not just a performer but also someone who appreciates the artistry behind music, often sharing songs, making playlists, and showing his personal taste to fans.
